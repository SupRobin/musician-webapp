What We Do

At Band Starter, we help musicians grow their brand, build real audiences, and get noticed. From visuals to strategy, our services are built to take artists to the next level.

We’ve worked with up-and-coming acts like The Static District, Moon Motel, and Razorview—delivering tailored campaigns that actually move the needle.

Branding & Identity

From logos to album covers and visual kits, we help define your look and message across all platforms. Our branding helped The Static District go from garage band to indie tour headliner.

Social Media Management

Don’t just post—strategize. We create and schedule content, build engagement, and help you grow fans who actually care. Our campaigns helped Moon Motel gain 20k+ followers in under 6 months.

Content Creation

Music videos, live sessions, vertical video reels, merch promo—we create content that looks legit and connects emotionally. Shot and edited in-house for full creative control.

Web & EPK Design

We build fast, mobile-optimized websites and electronic press kits (EPKs) that showcase your music, gigs, press, and more. Perfect for promoters, labels, and media contacts.

Digital Advertising

Targeted ads on YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, and Spotify to boost releases, tour stops, or merch drops. We helped Razorview double their pre-saves with one video ad campaign.

Release & Tour Promotion

We plan and promote your next release or tour stop-by-stop with artwork, press releases, digital flyers, and influencer coordination.
