About Us

Band Starter is a music marketing company that helps independent artists and bands grow their presence, connect with fans, and reach new audiences. We specialize in strategy, branding, and content that actually moves the needle.

Our History

Founded in 2020 by a team of musicians and marketers, Band Starter was born out of frustration with cookie-cutter marketing approaches. We’ve worked with over 75 artists across North America, helping them find their voice and their audience.

Our Mission

To empower artists to take control of their careers with authentic, strategic, and creative marketing support. We believe every great artist deserves to be heard—and we build the bridge to get there.
